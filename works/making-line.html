Making Line

x, 1982-1985

Performance Sculptures by Jo Wood Brown

A series of "Performance Sculptures" created by Jo Wood-Brown. Making Line was performed during the 20 to 30 minutes it took for plaster, the main element in these works, to change from a liquid to a solid. Jo's movements were trapped in the plaster and could be broken up and taken home..
