Incubatio Installation

5 Myles Art Gallery, Brooklyn, 2013

x by Jo Wood Brown, x by Miriam Parker

Incubatio installation, a 3-day multimedia installation situates as an urban refuge for dreaming, it includes live performances that place the viewer within a montage of film, sculpture, dance, music and poetry.
