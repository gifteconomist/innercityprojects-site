The Station

Whitebox Art Space, NYC, 2014

Installation by Jo Wood Brown, performance by Miriam Parker

The Station was a two-hour immersive live installation of sound, light, movement, and sculpture that explored how we form our world through images.

In The Station, the viewer becomes immersed in an interactive performance of dance and sculpture, integrating reality through the lens of the imagination. The images represent paintings and studio experiments from the evolving collaboration between Jo Wood-Brown and Miriam Parker.
